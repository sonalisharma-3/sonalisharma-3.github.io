# **Hello and welcome to my website!**

I am a Fulbright Scholar and researcher at Stanford University in the Artificial Intelligence and Medical Imaging Center, where I focus on the transparency and equitable integration of artificial intelligence (AI) in healthcare. My research explores how large language models (LLMs) and visionâ€“language models (VLMs) reason in clinical contexts, with particular emphasis on interpretability, safety, and alignment in radiology and medical imaging.

My main project is creating one of the first global human chain-of-thought and spatial datasets. By recording real-time chain-of-thought reasoning, spatial attention patterns, and interpretive pathways during chest X-ray analysis we can ground reasoning in the actual cognitive and visual processes of human radiologists.
